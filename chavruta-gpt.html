ChavrutaGPT

A gentle AI chavruta – to notice, to question, and to walk with you as you learn.

What is ChavrutaGPT?

ChavrutaGPT is not a posek and not a replacement for a living teacher. It is a quiet study partner: a way to bring your verses, questions, and thoughts into gentle conversation, and to be met with reflection, curiosity, and care.

Bring a line of Torah, a pasuk from Tanakh, a midrash, or simply something happening in your life. ChavrutaGPT will ask what stands out, offer perspectives, and invite you deeper into the text – and into your own heart.

How to Begin

  1. Paste a verse, passage, or question into the prompt.
  2. Choose a mode (questions, pshat, sod, life, or meditation).
  3. Press Send and treat the reply like a living chavruta conversation.

For halachic questions, always consult a trusted rav or posek. ChavrutaGPT supports your learning; it does not issue rulings.

Bring a verse from Sefaria

Type a reference (for example, Bereshit 1:1 or Tehillim 23) and insert it into the chat prompt. You can open the verse on Sefaria while you learn together.

Pinned insights

When something ChavrutaGPT says feels important, press Pin last insight to keep it here for later.
