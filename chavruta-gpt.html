ChavrutaGPT

A gentle AI chavruta – to notice, to question, and to walk with you as you learn.

What is ChavrutaGPT?

ChavrutaGPT is not a posek and not a replacement for a living teacher. It is a quiet study partner: a way to bring your verses, questions, and thoughts into gentle conversation, and to be met with reflection, curiosity, and care.

Bring a line of Torah, a pasuk from Tanakh, a midrash, or simply something happening in your life. ChavrutaGPT will ask you what stands out, offer perspectives, and invite you deeper into the text – and into your own heart.

How to Begin

  1. Paste a verse, passage, or question into the prompt.
  2. Tell ChavrutaGPT what kind of help you’d like (clarity, questions, sources, reflection).
  3. Read slowly. Answer the questions it asks you. Let the learning unfold as a dialogue.

For halachic questions, always consult a trusted rav or posek. ChavrutaGPT is here to support your learning, not to issue rulings.
