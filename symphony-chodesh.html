Symphony Chodesh

A monthly musical meditation woven around the Hebrew calendar and its letters.

The Idea

Each Hebrew month carries a different flavor — a letter, a tribe, a constellation, a subtle invitation in the heart. Symphony Chodesh is a way of listening to that invitation through sound: a monthly “track” that reflects the energies of the time.

This page can link to playlists, generated music, recorded niggunim, or your own compositions.

Prototype Player

In the future, this could be wired to a real audio player or generator. For now, treat it as a ritual: when you press “Play,” take a breath, say a blessing for the new month, and imagine the music you would like to hear.

Under the hood, you can later attach an actual <audio> element, streaming URL, or generative music API.
