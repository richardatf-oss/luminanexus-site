How this gateway works

Think of this page as a small map taped to the trunk of the Tree. Each card below opens a main doorway in Sefaria’s own library. From there, you can wander freely.

1 · Start from the main library

The Texts page on Sefaria lists all major categories: Tanakh, Talmud, Midrash, Halakhah, Kabbalah, liturgy, and more.

Visit Sefaria Texts →

2 · Follow the categories

Within each category (for example Tanakh or Talmud), Sefaria lets you choose books, commentaries, modern translations, and visualizations.

Try exploring Tanakh or Talmud directly:

  • Tanakh · Hebrew Bible
  • Talmud · Bavli & Yerushalmi

3 · Let citations become windows

Anywhere on LuminaNexus where we write references like Berakhot 2a or Genesis 1:1, the Sefaria Linker will turn them into pop-ups and links back to the full text.

(Hover or tap those citations on our pages to see the Sefaria preview.)
